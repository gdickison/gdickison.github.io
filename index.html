GAM, NOUN - a social meeting of two (or more) Whaleships, generally on a crusing ground; when, after exchanging hails, they exchange visits by boats' crews, the two captains remaining, for the time, on board of one ship, and the two chief mates on the other.
Herman Melville, Moby-Dick
